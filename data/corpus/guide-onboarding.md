# New Hire Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Meridian Mutual. This guide covers your first week.

## Day 1

- **Arrive at 8:30 a.m.** at Milwaukee Center HQ, 12th floor reception, or your assigned campus. Remote-first hires receive a separate FedEx shipment with hardware and documentation.
- **Photo and badge** issued by Security.
- **IT pickup** at the floor 12 IT bar — a MacBook Pro, monitor, and YubiKey.
- **Workday tasks** must be completed by end of business: I-9 Section 1 with documents present, federal/state withholdings (W-4 and state equivalent), direct deposit, and beneficiary designations for life and 401(k).

## Required Compliance Training (Week 1)

Assigned in Workday Learning, due within 7 calendar days:

- Code of Conduct
- Information Security Standards (Standard MM-IS-04)
- Anti-Money Laundering Awareness
- FINRA Basics (registered roles only)
- Privacy and PII Handling

## People Connections

- **30/60/90 check-ins** with your manager — the template is auto-loaded into your Workday Goals.
- **Buddy program** — your people partner assigns a buddy in your first 3 days.
- **Department orientation** — usually held the first Friday morning of each month.

## Benefits Enrollment

You have **30 days** from your start date to make benefit elections. Defaults will apply if you don't elect; HR Benefits cannot accept late elections except for qualifying life events.

## Resources

- **HR Hub** at hrhub.meridianmutual.com — single source for forms and policies
- **ServiceNow** for IT requests
- **People Partner** for org-specific questions; find yours in Workday under My Team > People Partner

## First-Month Milestones

By day 30: complete all compliance training, finalize benefits, hold first 1:1 with your manager, and attend at least one cross-team meeting.

For questions, contact HR Operations at hrops@meridianmutual.com.
