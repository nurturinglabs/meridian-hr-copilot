# Parental Leave Policy

Meridian Mutual provides 8 weeks of paid parental leave to eligible employees following the birth or adoption of a child. This policy is effective January 1, 2022.

## Eligibility

Full-time employees who have completed twelve months of continuous service are eligible. Primary caregivers receive the full 8 weeks; secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks. Designation of primary versus secondary caregiver is made by the employee in writing prior to leave start.

## Pay and Duration

The 8 weeks are paid at 100% of base salary and must be taken continuously within the first 6 months following the qualifying event. Birthing parents may also use up to 6 weeks of short-term disability, coordinated by Lincoln Financial.

## How to Request Leave

1. Notify your manager at least 60 days before the expected leave start date when foreseeable.
2. Submit Form MM-PL-100 via the legacy HR Portal (PeopleSoft).
3. Attach physician's expected delivery date or adoption placement order.
4. HR Benefits will confirm eligibility within 10 business days.

## Coordination with Other Leave

Parental leave runs concurrently with FMLA and any applicable state leave programs. Employees must exhaust accrued PTO and sick time before parental leave begins.

## Return to Work

Employees return to a position of comparable responsibility and pay. On-site lactation rooms are available at the Milwaukee, Brookfield, and New York offices.

For questions, contact HR Benefits at benefits@meridianmutual.com or extension 4400.
