# Parental Leave Policy

Meridian Mutual provides 12 weeks of paid parental leave to eligible employees following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. This policy supersedes the prior 2022 policy and is effective January 1, 2025.

## Eligibility

Full-time and benefits-eligible part-time employees who have completed six months of continuous service are eligible. Both birthing and non-birthing parents qualify. The leave applies regardless of gender, marital status, or whether the employee is the primary caregiver.

## Pay and Duration

The 12 weeks are paid at 100% of base salary and may be taken continuously or, with manager approval, intermittently within the first 12 months following the qualifying event. Birthing parents may also use up to 6 additional weeks of short-term disability for medical recovery, coordinated by Lincoln Financial.

## How to Request Leave

1. Notify your manager at least 30 days before the expected leave start date when foreseeable.
2. Submit Form MM-PL-100 via Workday under Time Off → Parental Leave.
3. Attach supporting documentation: physician's expected delivery date, adoption placement order, or foster care order.
4. HR Benefits will confirm eligibility within 5 business days.

## Coordination with Other Leave

Parental leave runs concurrently with FMLA and applicable state programs (e.g., Wisconsin BMLA, New York PFL). Employees are not required to exhaust accrued PTO before taking parental leave.

## Return to Work

Employees return to the same position or one of equivalent pay and responsibility. Lactation rooms are available at every Meridian Mutual office; remote employees may expense a Milk Stork shipping benefit during business travel for up to 12 months following return.

For questions, contact HR Benefits at benefits@meridianmutual.com or extension 4400.
