# Health Insurance — FAQ

## What plans are offered?

Meridian Mutual offers three medical plans through **Aetna**:

- **PPO Plus** — $500 individual deductible, $35 PCP copay, broad network
- **HSA Saver** — $2,500 individual deductible, no copays, paired with an HSA
- **HMO Local** — $0 deductible, requires PCP referral, available in WI and NY only

Dental coverage is through **Delta Dental**, vision through **VSP**.

## When can I enroll or make changes?

Open enrollment runs **November 1 through November 15** each year. Outside open enrollment, you have **30 days** following a qualifying life event (marriage, birth or adoption, divorce, loss of other coverage, change in dependent eligibility) to make changes via the Workday Life Event task.

## What does Meridian Mutual contribute to the HSA?

For employees who elect HSA Saver, the company contributes:

- **$1,000** to individual coverage
- **$2,000** to family coverage

Contributions are made in two installments (January and July). Employees may add their own pre-tax contributions up to IRS limits.

## Pharmacy

Prescription benefits are administered by **Express Scripts**. Maintenance medications must be filled via mail order or at Walgreens after the third 30-day fill at retail.

## Mental Health

Behavioral health is offered through **Spring Health**. The first 8 sessions per year are at no cost; subsequent sessions follow your medical plan's behavioral health benefit. Access at meridian.springhealth.com.

## Telehealth

Teladoc visits are $0 on the PPO Plus and HMO Local plans, and subject to the deductible on HSA Saver.

## Where can I find detailed costs?

Plan summaries (SBCs), premium tables, and the provider lookup are posted on HR Hub > Benefits > Health.

For questions, contact HR Benefits at benefits@meridianmutual.com or extension 4400.
