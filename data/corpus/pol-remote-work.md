# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

Meridian Mutual operates a hybrid model. Most office-based roles work **3 days in-office and 2 days remote**, with on-site days set by department.

## Fully Remote Work

A small number of roles are designated fully remote at hire. Existing employees may request a change to fully remote with VP-level approval and a completed **Form MM-RW-200**. Approval depends on role licensure (FINRA-registered roles cannot be remote in non-supervised states), client-facing requirements, and team composition. Decisions are reviewed annually each March.

## Equipment and Stipend

Meridian Mutual provides a MacBook Pro and a 27-inch external monitor. Remote and hybrid employees receive:

- A **$500 ergonomic stipend** in their first year (chair, desk, lighting), reimbursable through Concur with category "Home Office Setup"
- **$200 annually** thereafter for replacement or upgrades
- Mobile reimbursement of **$50/month** for roles with on-call rotations

## Home Office Expectations

- A dedicated, lockable workspace when handling client PII or NPI under our Information Security Standard MM-IS-04
- WPA3 or WPA2-Enterprise WiFi; the IT-managed VPN must be active during work hours
- A camera-on default for client and cross-team meetings

## Hours and Availability

Working hours are 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. local time, with reasonable flex for personal appointments. Remote employees should overlap at least 6 hours with their manager's time zone.

## In-Person Connection

Each team holds at least one **in-person team gathering per quarter**, typically at the Milwaukee or Manhattan campus. Travel and lodging are covered per the Expense Report SOP.

## Tax and Workers' Comp

Employees must keep their state of work current in Workday. Working from a non-registered state for more than 30 days requires HR approval and may have payroll tax implications.

For questions, contact HR Operations at hrops@meridianmutual.com.
