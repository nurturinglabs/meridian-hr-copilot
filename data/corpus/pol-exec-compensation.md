# Executive Compensation Policy

This policy governs the compensation of Meridian Mutual executives at the **Vice President level and above**. It is reviewed annually by the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors with support from the company's independent compensation consultant.

## Total Direct Compensation

Executive total direct compensation has three components: base salary, short-term incentive (STI), and long-term incentive (LTI).

## Base Salary

Base salaries are positioned to the **median of the financial-services peer group** (the "Peer Set" maintained by the Committee, currently 18 companies). Annual review occurs at the March Committee meeting.

## Short-Term Incentive

The STI plan is annual cash, paid in March of the following year. Targets by tier:

- VP: **30% of base**
- SVP: **45% of base**
- EVP / NEO: **60–80% of base**

Funding is determined by **company performance** (60%; metrics: net operating gain, AUM growth, expense ratio) and **individual performance** (40%; goal scorecard reviewed by the CEO and the Committee). A modifier of 0×–2× applies.

## Long-Term Incentive

LTI is granted annually on **March 1**:

- **60% Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)** — three-year cliff vest
- **40% Performance Stock Units (PSUs)** — three-year performance period, payout 0–200% based on relative TSR versus the Peer Set

Equity awards are administered by Fidelity Stock Plan Services and are subject to the company's **Insider Trading and Window Period Policy** (Form MM-LE-101).

## Clawback

Cash and equity incentives are subject to recovery under our **Section 10D / Dodd-Frank-aligned clawback policy** in the event of a material accounting restatement, fraud, or significant misconduct. The policy applies to current and former executive officers for three years.

## Deferred Compensation

Executives with base salary above **$200,000** are eligible for the Meridian Mutual Deferred Compensation Plan (NQDC). Elections are made in November for the following plan year and are irrevocable.

## Change in Control

Equity awards include **double-trigger acceleration**. Cash severance benefits, where applicable, are subject to **280G best-net** treatment.

For questions, contact executive.comp@meridianmutual.com or your assigned Compensation Director.
