# Benefits Enrollment FAQ — Common Examples

This FAQ uses worked examples — with realistic-looking participant data — to illustrate common enrollment scenarios. Names and identifiers in these examples are fictional.

## Q: What information do I need to enroll dependents in my health plan?

A: You'll need each dependent's full legal name, **date of birth**, and **Social Security number**.

For example, when **John Doe (SSN 123-45-6789, DOB 01/15/1980)** enrolled his spouse **Jane Doe (SSN 987-65-4321, DOB 03/22/1982)** in PPO Plus, he submitted both records via the Workday Life Event task within 30 days of their wedding. The dental and vision elections carried automatically.

## Q: How do I update my HSA direct deposit?

A: Submit **Form MM-HSA-301** with your bank routing number and account number.

For example, employee **Maria Patel** updated her HSA contributions to route to her personal checking **account ending 7788**, replacing the previous **account ending 4321** that was closed when she changed banks. The change took effect the following pay period.

## Q: My corporate AmEx was lost — what do I do for medical reimbursements while it's replaced?

A: Report the card immediately to Corporate Card Services at extension 4900. While a replacement is issued, employees may temporarily charge medical out-of-pocket items to a personal card and submit through Concur.

For example, employee **Robert Chen** submitted a $148.32 urgent-care receipt charged to his personal card **4111-1111-1111-1111** while his replacement AmEx was in transit; reimbursement processed in 5 business days.

## Q: What documentation is needed for a qualifying life event?

A: A marriage certificate, birth certificate, or proof-of-loss-of-coverage letter, depending on the event.

For example, employee **Lisa Romero** submitted her child's birth certificate (**DOB 06/12/2025**) within 21 days to add the child to her dental plan retroactive to the date of birth.

## Q: How do I update beneficiary information on my 401(k)?

A: Update via Fidelity NetBenefits. For each beneficiary, provide full legal name, **SSN**, and **date of birth**.

For example, **John Doe (SSN 123-45-6789)** designated his daughter **Emma Doe (DOB 09/04/2010)** as primary beneficiary at 60% and his son **Liam Doe (DOB 11/22/2013)** at 40%.

For questions, contact HR Benefits at benefits@meridianmutual.com.
