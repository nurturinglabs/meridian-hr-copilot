# Expense Report Submission — Standard Operating Procedure

## Scope

This SOP covers business expenses incurred by employees of Meridian Mutual that are eligible for reimbursement.

## Submission Window

Expenses must be submitted in **Concur** within **30 days** of the date incurred. Reports older than 90 days are automatically declined and require VP approval to override.

## Receipts

A scanned or photographed receipt is required for any single line item **greater than $25**. Receipts must show vendor name, date, amount, and last four of the payment card. For meals, attendee names and business purpose are required regardless of amount.

## Mileage

Personal-vehicle business mileage is reimbursed at the **2026 IRS standard rate of $0.67 per mile**. Submit using Concur's mileage tool with start address, end address, and business purpose. Commute mileage is not reimbursable.

## Meals

Daily meal limits per traveler:

- Breakfast: **$35**
- Lunch: **$50**
- Dinner: **$75**
- Alcohol: **not reimbursable** under any circumstance, including with clients (a separate Client Entertainment process exists — Form MM-FN-310)

For trips of 3+ nights, the per-diem option (US GSA rates) may be elected in lieu of receipt-based meal claims.

## Corporate AmEx

The Corporate AmEx is required for travel and is the default payment method for airfare, lodging, and ground transportation. Out-of-pocket charges should be the exception. Card balances unpaid for more than 60 days will be flagged to the employee's manager.

## Approval Flow

1. Employee submits in Concur
2. Manager approves within 5 business days
3. Finance review for policy compliance
4. Reimbursement via direct deposit, typically within 7 business days of final approval

## Rejected Items

Items rejected by Finance must be resubmitted within **7 days** with corrected documentation, or the employee accepts the charge personally.

For questions, contact Finance Operations at expenses@meridianmutual.com.
